Divya Wants to complete the Dream, for which she need to complete the level.
To help her with that there are generators which will generate BRIDGE and SLING for her.
To make the Sling click the S alphabet then carry the orb to the generator and repeat for L, I, N, and G.
Then the sling can be use the generator which will form the Bridge.

CONTROLS:
A- Left Movement
D- Right Movement
Space- To hold the orb
Again Space to release the orb

Remember to release the orb before using Sling
Mouse click and Drag to use the Sling
